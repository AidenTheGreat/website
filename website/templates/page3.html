About The Founders

Aiden Ackerman

Aiden is the coder of this website. Coming from a family of immigrants and being a first generation American citizen, Aiden understands the struggles of finding affordable products, education, and especially housing. Through perserverance and hard work, he worked his way into a scholarship at a private school. Now he wants to give back to the city that has taken him in and helped him so much.

Solayman Araba

As a resident of Manhattan in New York City, he has seen, firsthand, the wealth disparities between people, from those who are reduced to living on the street and in public housing, all the way to living in Millionaire's Row. Seeing the deplorable conditions in places like NYCHA and also noticing how people are overpaying for such low quality apartments. Noticing all of this, he has decided to dedicate his time to the cause of beautification within New York City apartments.

Chris Mitchell

As a New Yorker living in the perpetually underserved and overlooked community of Southeast Queens, Christopher has seen firsthand the problems that arise when lower-income communities aren't given even resources or attention to thrive. A bus-ride away from his residence is Jamaica Estates: the birthplace of billionaires--but if you go down the nearby hill you'll notice New Yorkers failed by a broken system put in place to produce generation upon generation of renters reliant upon public assistance/housing. Through partnership, collaboration, and advocacy, Christopher wants to give back to the city that took him in.
